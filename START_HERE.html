Optional Hardware
📡
SDR Receiver — Live ADS-B without internet
RTL-SDR dongle (~$25) receives real aircraft radio signals on 1090 MHz. No internet needed. Perfect for field trips and camping.
pip install pyModeS flask flask-cors
python3 hardware/sdr_receiver.py --lat 48.86 --lon 2.35
→ In app: tap 📡 → Connect → http://localhost:8090
🔌
BBC micro:bit — Physical control tower
LED radar, plane counter, compass pointer, buzzer alerts. V2 recommended. Chrome/Edge only (Web Bluetooth).
Flash hardware/microbit_makecode.js to micro:bit
→ In app: tap 🔌 → Select via Bluetooth

Troubleshooting
No planes?
Wait 10 sec, or press D for demo mode
Location denied?
Pick a city from the list instead
Black sky?
It's nighttime at your location
No sound?
Tap the 🔇 button to enable audio
micro:bit won't pair?
Use Chrome or Edge (Web Bluetooth)
SDR can't reach?
Check that sdr_receiver.py is running
For Teachers
Email index.html to students — it just works. No accounts, no logins, no data collected. Five modes form a natural lesson progression. Covers geography, math, science, history, and coding in one app. Heritage mode connects Islamic Golden Age scholars to live STEM. Students can read and modify the source code. See the FAQ for classroom lesson ideas.
